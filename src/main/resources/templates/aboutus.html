About us

GoGreen is an online nursery store that specializes in selling a variety of indoor and outdoor plants, gardening supplies, and accessories. The store offers a wide range of plant categories, including succulents, herbs, fruit trees, and more. GoGreen aims to provide high-quality, healthy plants that are shipped directly to customers' doorsteps. They also offer a 30-day plant guarantee, which allows customers to receive a replacement or a refund if a plant arrives in poor condition. GoGreen provides helpful resources and guides on its website for plant care and gardening tips, and offers a plant subscription service that delivers a new plant to customers each month.
